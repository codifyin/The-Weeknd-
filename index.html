About

Learn more about The Weeknd

Canadian singer & songwriter

The Weeknd is the first artist to surpass 100 million monthly listeners in Spotify history.

  • Birthday: 16 February 1990
  • Early life: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
  • Debut Studio Album: "Kiss Land", 2013
  • Awards: 54
  • Age: 33
  • Musical beginnings: 2010
  • Mainstream Success: "Beauty Behind the Madness", 2015
  • Acting career: 2019 film "Uncut Gems"

Prior to these mainstream milestones, singer Abel Tesfaye entered the music industry in an unorthodox way. His origin story includes anonymous YouTube videos, mysterious mixtapes, and internet hype.
